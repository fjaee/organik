Today's Pick

Cisco NetConnect

College of Computer and Information Sciences

The Cisco NetConnect - PUP Manila student organization is a community committed to shaping the next generation of tech leaders and empowering members with the skills and knowledge necessary in networking, cybersecurity, and other related fields.

At Cisco PUP, we bridge the gap between academic learning and the professional world, bringing together passionate individuals eager to push boundaries. We create opportunities for people to apply their existing knowledge in real-world scenarios, preparing them to face future challenges in the IT industry. We embrace creativity and help members explore new ideas and grow their skills while fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where they can build a network of peers.

Through hands-on learning experiences like workshops and collaborative projects, Cisco PUP encourages innovation and allows everyone to develop practical skills that set them apart in the competitive tech world. Our commitment to quality and continual improvement ensures we strive to achieve the highest standards in all aspects of our work.
